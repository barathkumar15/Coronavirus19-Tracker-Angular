SYMPTOMS OF CORONAVIRUS

People with COVID-19 have had a wide range of symptoms reported – from mild to severe. Symptoms may appear 2–14 days after exposure to the virus.

Fever or chills Cough Shortness of breath Fatigue Muscle or body aches Loss of taste or smell Sore throat Nausea / Vomiting Diarrhea

This list is not exhaustive. Please refer to CDC or consult a doctor for evolving symptoms.

When to Seek Emergency Medical Attention

Watch for these emergency warning signs. Seek medical attention immediately if someone is experiencing:

  • 🫁 Trouble breathing
  • ❤️ Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  • 😵 New confusion
  • 💤 Inability to wake or stay awake
  • 💙 Bluish lips or face

*This list is not complete. Contact a healthcare provider for any other severe or concerning symptoms.

📞 Call 911 or your local emergency service if necessary.
